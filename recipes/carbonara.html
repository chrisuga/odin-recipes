Carbonara

The Italian pasta dish called "Carbonara" is cooked with bacon and eggs, with common additions of cheese, onions, and other common breakfast omelette additions.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • cheese

Steps

  1. boil pasta
  2. fry bacon
  3. combine pasta and bacon with oil
  4. add eggs, cook with heat
  5. add cheese
