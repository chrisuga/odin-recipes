Tonkatsu

This fried pork dish is hearty and tastes great. All it takes is a little bit of leftover oil to turn boring old pork into fantastic fried goodness!

Ingredients

  • pork cutlets
  • eggs
  • flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • frying oil
  1. coat pork in flour
  2. combine eggs nad oil
  3. coat pork in egg-oil mixture
  4. prepare frying pan while pork sets
  5. fry pork in oil for 2-3 minutes per side
  6. let sit and degrease
